About Equinox 2023

Equinox Is The Annual Inter-Collegiate Techno-Cultural Fest Hosted By Lourdes Matha College of Science and Technology.

Welcome to our annual college fest, a celebration of creativity, talent, and community spirit. This year's event promises to be bigger and better than ever before, with a range of exciting activities and performances lined up for you.

Our college fest is an opportunity for students to showcase their skills and talents across a range of domains, including music, dance, drama, arts, and sports. It is also a chance for students to come together and enjoy a fun-filled day of entertainment, socializing, and networking.

Our fest is organized by a team of dedicated volunteers from the student community, along with faculty members and staff. We have put in months of hard work and planning to create an unforgettable experience for everyone.

We invite all students, faculty, staff, and members of the local community to join us for this exciting event. Come and be a part of the festivities and experience the vibrant energy and enthusiasm of our college community.
